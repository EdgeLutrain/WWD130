The submission and comission process
  1. You will submit the commission requests to me via Instagram dm. With reference picture(s) of the Character/Subject wanting to be drawn. along with tell me what pose/situation they want the character in.
  2. I will either approve or deny their request after consideration
  3. After discussing what they want (sketch, lineart ect.), how detailed the character is, and how complex the pose will be, I will tell them how much it will cost.
  4. Half of the payment upfront, other half after it's been completed
